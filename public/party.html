Major Parties

Prosperity Party

ብልጽግና ፓርቲ

Ideology

Medemer (Synergy): Emphasizes collective action, unity, and economic liberalization while maintaining federal structure.

Leader
Prime Minister Abiy Ahmed

ጠቅላይ ሚኒስትር ዐቢይ አሕመድ

Description

Founded in December 2019 by merging former ruling coalition components. Won large majority in 2021 election.

በታኅሣሥ 2012 ዓ.ም. የተመሰረተ ገዥ ፓርቲ ነው። በ2013 ዓ.ም. ምርጫ ከፍተኛ መቀመጫ አሸንፏል።

Ethiopian Citizens for Social Justice

EZEMA (ኢዜማ)

Ideology

Social Justice, Liberal Democracy: Champions civic nationalism and liberalism based on individual citizenship.

Leader
Berhanu Nega

በርሃኑ ነጋ

Description

Major opposition party formed in May 2019. Largest opposition party in Parliament.

በግንቦት 2011 ዓ.ም. በርካታ ተቃዋሚዎችን በማዋሃድ የተመሰረተ ዋና ተቃዋሚ ፓርቲ ነው። በፓርላማ ትልቁ የተቃዋሚ ፓርቲ ነው።

National Movement of Amhara

NaMA (ናማ)

Ideology

Amhara Nationalism: Focuses on protecting Amhara historical rights, identity, and security.

Leader
Desalagne Chanie

ደሳላኝ ቻኒዬ

Description

Established in June 2018. Strong influence in Amhara region with federal parliament seats.

በሰኔ 2010 ዓ.ም. የተቋቋመ ክልላዊ/ብሔራዊ ፓርቲ ነው። በአማራ ክልል ከፍተኛ ተፅዕኖ ፈጣሪ ነው።

Oromo Federalist Congress

OFC (ኦፌሲ)

Ideology

Ethnic Federalism & Oromo Self-Rule: Seeks greater autonomy and equitable resource sharing for Oromo people.

Leader
Obbo Merera Gudina

ኦቦ መረራ ጉዲና

Description

Long-standing Oromo-based opposition group founded in 2012. Boycotted 2021 election.

በ2004 ዓ.ም. በውህደት የተመሰረተ የረዥም ጊዜ የኦሮሞ ተቃዋሚ ቡድን ነው። በ2013 ዓ.ም. ምርጫ አልተሳተፈም።
